Chole Bhature

Description

Chole Bhature is a classic and beloved dish in North Indian cuisine.

Chole refers to spicy chickpea curry, while Bhature are deep-fried bread made from refined flour. Together, they create a hearty and indulgent meal.

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas (Chole)
  • Refined flour (Maida)
  • Baking powder
  • Yogurt
  • Oil (for frying Bhature)
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Green chilies
  • Spices
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Lemon
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Soak Chickpeas
  2. Prepare Dough
  3. Cook Chickpeas
  4. Prepare Chole
  5. Shape and Fry Bhature
  6. Serve
