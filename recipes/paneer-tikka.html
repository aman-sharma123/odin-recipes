Paneer Tikka with Naan

Description

Paneer Tikka with Naan is a quintessential Indian appetizer or main course, featuring marinated cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) and vegetables grilled to perfection. The paneer and vegetables are marinated in a mixture of yogurt, spices, and lemon juice, imparting a burst of flavor and tenderness to each bite. Served alongside freshly baked naan bread brushed with butter, this dish offers a harmonious blend of smoky, spicy, and buttery flavors.

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Yogurt
  • Bell Peppers
  • Onions
  • Spices
  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic paste
  • Ginger paste
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Naan bread
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Cut paneer, bell peppers, and onions into cubes.
  2. Marinate paneer and vegetables in a mixture of yogurt, spices, lemon juice, garlic paste, ginger paste, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Skewer the marinated paneer and vegetables and grill or bake until cooked and slightly charred.
  4. Heat naan bread on a skillet or in the oven. Spread butter on top.
  5. Serve hot paneer tikka with freshly baked naan, garnished with chopped coriander leaves.
